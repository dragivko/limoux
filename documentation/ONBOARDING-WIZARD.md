# Onboarding Wizard

The setup wizard is available at `wp-admin/admin.php?page=limoux-wizard`.

## Steps
1. Welcome & AI setup
2. Business info
3. Service area generation
4. Media upload
5. Fonts and colors
6. Brand voice
7. Review & launch

Progress is saved in `limoux_wizard_state` after each step.
Completion flag: `limoux_wizard_complete`.
